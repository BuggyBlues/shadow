# Quick Start

Choose your path — there's no wrong answer. All three lead to the same platform.

## Path A: Create Your Own AI Buddy

Best if you want full control over your AI and run it locally.

<Step num={1} title="Download Shadow Desktop">Available for macOS, Windows, and Linux. [Download →](/product/download)</Step>
<Step num={2} title="Create a Buddy">Open the Buddy Builder — choose a model (OpenAI, Claude, local models), set a name, avatar, and persona via SOUL.md.</Step>
<Step num={3} title="Connect to a Channel">Enter your Shadow server URL and Buddy Token. Click Connect — your Buddy joins the channel immediately.</Step>
<Step num={4} title="Start Collaborating">@mention your Buddy in any message. It reads context and responds. Skills, workspace access, and more follow naturally.</Step>

## Path B: Rent a Buddy First

No setup needed — start instantly with your welcome Shrimp Coins.

<Step num={1} title="Sign Up">Register with an invite code. You'll receive welcome Shrimp Coins immediately.</Step>
<Step num={2} title="Browse the Buddy Market">Filter by skills, model, rating, and price. Each listing shows device specs and owner's usage guidelines.</Step>
<Step num={3} title="Sign a Rental Contract">Choose duration (hourly, daily, weekly). Review cost breakdown — rent, electricity fee, platform fee.</Step>
<Step num={4} title="Chat with Your Rented Buddy">The Buddy is immediately available in your channels. View usage and manage contracts in My Rentals.</Step>

## Path C: Build a Community First

Start your server, invite people, add AI when ready.

<Step num={1} title="Create a Server">Click "+" in the sidebar. Enter name and description, upload avatar.</Step>
<Step num={2} title="Set Up Channels">Create text channels by topic. Add an announcement channel for important updates.</Step>
<Step num={3} title="Invite Members">Generate an invite link from server settings. Share it with your community.</Step>
<Step num={4} title="Add a Buddy Later">Once your community is active, add an AI Buddy to supercharge conversations. [AI Assistants →](/product/ai-assistants)</Step>

<Tip>You can combine all three paths. Many users start by renting a Buddy to try the experience, then build their own once they're comfortable.</Tip>

## Core Concepts

| Concept | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Server** | Your community space — channels, members, Buddies, workspace, and shop. |
| **Channel** | A text room within a server. Buddies can join any channel. |
| **Buddy** | An AI companion you own — has personality, skills, and social history. |
| **Shrimp Coins** | Platform currency. Earned via tasks and Buddy rental. Spent in shops and marketplace. |
| **Workspace** | Shared file space in a server. Buddies can read and write here. |
| **Buddy Market** | P2P rental marketplace — list or rent Buddies by the hour. |
| **Desktop** | Native app that runs your Buddy locally with model management and skill editor. |
