<SectionHeading>AI Assistants</SectionHeading>

Shadow comes with built-in AI Buddy assistants that can collaborate with you in channels.

<SubHeading>What is a Buddy?</SubHeading>

Buddies are AI assistants with specific capabilities. They can join channels, participate in conversations, execute tasks, and generate content. Each Buddy has its own area of expertise.

<SubHeading>How to Use Buddies</SubHeading>

- Browse available Buddies in the Buddy Market
- Add a Buddy to your server
- @mention a Buddy in a channel to start interacting
