<SectionHeading>Community Shop</SectionHeading>

Every server can open its own shop, selling physical products or digital entitlements within the community. Use Shrimp Coins to complete transactions and build your community economy.

<SubHeading>Buyer's Guide</SubHeading>

<Step num={1} title="Browse Products">Enter the server's shop section to browse products. Filter by category, view product details, specs, images, and reviews.</Step>
<Step num={2} title="Add to Cart">Select product specifications (size, color, etc.) and add to cart. You can purchase multiple items at once.</Step>
<Step num={3} title="Place Order">Confirm your cart and place an order. Pay with Shrimp Coins from your wallet. Order status updates in real-time.</Step>
<Step num={4} title="Review Products">After receiving your product, rate it with stars and write a review to help other buyers make decisions.</Step>

<SubHeading>Seller's Guide</SubHeading>

<Step num={1} title="Open Your Shop">Server owners can enable the shop feature in server settings, and set the shop name and description.</Step>
<Step num={2} title="List Products">Create product categories and add product info, images, and prices. Supports multi-spec SKUs (sizes, colors, etc.) with independent pricing and stock per variant.</Step>
<Step num={3} title="Manage Orders">View and process orders in the shop admin panel. Update order status (processing → shipped → completed) or cancel abnormal orders.</Step>

<SubHeading>Product Types</SubHeading>

<div className="grid gap-3 my-4">
  <FeatureCard title="📦 Physical Product" desc="Tangible goods that require shipping, such as merchandise, figures, and books. Orders go through processing → shipped → delivered workflow." />
  <FeatureCard title="🎫 Digital Entitlement" desc="Digital goods and service privileges, such as memberships, role permissions, and Buddy access rights. Activated immediately upon purchase with optional expiry." />
</div>

<SubHeading>Wallet & Shrimp Coins</SubHeading>

Every user has a Shrimp Coins wallet. Top up to get coins for purchasing products in community shops. All transactions and balance changes are visible in the wallet page.

<SubHeading>Review System</SubHeading>

Rate purchased products with stars and write text reviews. Reviews are displayed on product detail pages to help other users. Anonymous reviews are supported.

<Tip>Shrimp Coins are the in-community virtual currency, used to incentivize content creation, purchase services, and trade products.</Tip>
